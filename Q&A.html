FAQ

If you are new to club or overlooking, the following guide will help you learn more about the club and how it works.

Where and When is the rally taken place?

The rally are taken place in five countries that we extented: UK, Germany, Spain, Italy and France and our events are usually held in Winter, Summer and Autumn.



What if the rally have to cancel due to COVID-19?

Don't worry! We will announced when COVID-19 regulations require us to change or delay the date of the event. We will support virtual online show and you can participate and we will refund all ticket bookers



Can I purchase tickets at the show entrance?

Yes, tickets will be available to purchase at the box office. But, you cannot get extra promotion or discount and you will also pay taxes if you haven't booked. Booking is recommended as as a result of COVID-19 guidelines and to ensure we maintain social distancing throughout the event.



Is the event in Low Emission Zone?

Yes, the events are in a Low Emission Zone. Anyone driving in a car or bike will not have to pay any charges in these areas.



Parking free?

Mostly we provide parking but is limited. So, you can order parking in booking.
