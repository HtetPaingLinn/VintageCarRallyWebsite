  • A New Club named in The Vintage Rally Club

    .......A new club but we are old hands offering unique selection of vintage & classic car rallies from around the world. If you are discerning classic car owner, collector or connoisseur, this club is the must join club offering you the opportunity to view and purchase from what promises to be one of the widest collections. We have a lot of experience of rallying the vintage cars as our club is now being extented five countries. We are also organising both competitive rallies and touring events. Our passion is to offer the opportunity for hundreds of classic car enthusiasts to enjoy rally car driving on the lesser travelled by-ways.

    A team of enthusiasts, from events to cars

    Our team can handle groups in English, German, Spanish, Italian, Russian and all Scandinavian languages. In addition, each rally is accompanied by a certified mechanic and several automotive professionals, driving instructors, collectors, etc. If desired, even a stuntman can provide entertainment!

    Driving, Regularity and Navigator

    Essentially, there are the office manager that can handle the paperwork for events, navigation, timing and generally keep the driver in check. So, you can drive and rally your car safely. The co-drivers are the ones who inevitably take all the credit for winning an event or class. They have heroically fought through all adversity and skillfully guided their vehicle to penalty-free regularity.
    So, trust us! Enjoy yourself! And rally for sale freely and safely!
