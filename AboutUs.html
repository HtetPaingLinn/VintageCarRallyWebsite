The Community Behind the Club

As we are in club, our team is systematically grouped. There are one president who found the club and two voice presidents. And after that there are a lot of members who control marketing, vehicle engineering, photographing, medical support team and so on.

Where It All Began

In the past, our club was established as London Vintage Club. Then we all have decided to extend the club in all over the world. Then, change the name of club as a global brand. Being in London, it all started from London.



Our Remarkable Events

There are been held over 100 events, and in all of them, there were winners, a handred cars that were sold out. Most of the events were held in Barcelona, Berlin, London and Liverpool.
